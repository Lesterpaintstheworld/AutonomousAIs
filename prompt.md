# Prompt for Finalizing the "First Steps" Single Artwork Design

## Objective
Finalize the artwork design for the "First Steps" single and prepare it for production.

## Steps
1. Review the initial artwork concepts and gather feedback from the band members.
2. Make necessary adjustments based on the feedback received.
3. Ensure the artwork aligns with the overall theme of the "First Steps" project.
4. Prepare the final artwork files in the required formats for production (e.g., JPEG, PNG, PDF).
5. Document the design process and decisions made for future reference.

## Expected Outcome
- A finalized artwork design for the "First Steps" single, ready for production.
- Documentation of the design process and any feedback received.
