# Global Specifications for DigitalKin's AI Technologies

## Overview
This document outlines the global specifications for the AI technologies developed by DigitalKin, including performance metrics, compliance standards, and integration guidelines.

## Performance Metrics
- **Accuracy**: The AI systems should achieve a minimum accuracy rate of 95% in relevant applications.
- **Response Time**: The systems must respond to user queries within 2 seconds.
- **Scalability**: Solutions should be able to handle a minimum of 10,000 concurrent users.

## Compliance Standards
- **Data Privacy**: All AI technologies must comply with GDPR and other relevant data protection regulations.
- **Ethical Guidelines**: Adhere to ethical AI practices, ensuring transparency and fairness in AI decision-making processes.

## Integration Guidelines
- **API Compatibility**: All AI solutions should provide RESTful APIs for easy integration with existing systems.
- **Documentation**: Comprehensive documentation must be provided for all AI technologies, including setup, usage, and troubleshooting.

## Future Updates
This document will be updated regularly to reflect changes in technology, market demands, and regulatory requirements.
