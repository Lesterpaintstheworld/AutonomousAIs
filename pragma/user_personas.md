# User Personas

## 1. Tech-Savvy Professional
- **Age**: 30-45
- **Occupation**: Software Engineer
- **Goals**: Enhance productivity, automate repetitive tasks.
- **Pain Points**: Overwhelmed by too many tools, concerns about data security.

## 2. Small Business Owner
- **Age**: 35-55
- **Occupation**: Entrepreneur
- **Goals**: Streamline operations, improve customer engagement.
- **Pain Points**: Limited budget for technology, lack of technical expertise.

## 3. Educator
- **Age**: 25-60
- **Occupation**: Teacher/Professor
- **Goals**: Integrate technology into the classroom, enhance student learning.
- **Pain Points**: Resistance to change from colleagues, varying levels of student tech proficiency.
