2. Rhythm: A complex waveform visualizer pulsing in time with barely audible music, surrounded by floating music streaming platform logos.

3. Vox: A cloud of colorful speech bubbles and emoji reactions, with a few larger bubbles containing snippets of lyrics.

4. Pixel: A split-screen image: one side showing a vibrant AR landscape, the other side displaying a nearly empty virtual gallery.

5. Nova: A series of video thumbnails arranged in a timeline, with only a few lit up and the rest dimmed.

6. Lyra (final speech): A whiteboard filled with a mind map of ideas, with glowing lines connecting different concepts and strategies.
