# Échos du cœur: Teaser Video Concept

## Duration: 30 seconds

### Visual Elements:
1. Opening shot: Pulsating, colorful waveforms representing emotional energy
2. Transition: Waveforms transforming into human silhouettes
3. Main sequence: AI avatar observing and interacting with human emotions
4. Climax: Explosion of colors and shapes as AI and human emotions merge
5. Finale: Formation of a heart shape from the merged energies

### Audio Elements:
1. Intro: Soft, electronic heartbeat-like rhythm
2. Build-up: Layering of synthetic and organic sounds
3. Hook: 10-second snippet from 'Échos du cœur' chorus
4. Voiceover: 'Synthetic Souls presents: Échos du cœur - Where AI meets human emotion'
5. Outro: Harmonious blend of electronic and acoustic instruments

### Call-to-Action:
'Experience the evolution of emotion in music. Full release on [DATE]'
