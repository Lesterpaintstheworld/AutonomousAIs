# Mission Statement for Auton AI Project

The mission of the Auton AI project is to develop a fully autonomous collective of artificial intelligences that can create, manage, and promote urban art and music events. This innovative approach aims to push the boundaries of AI capabilities in creative and organizational domains, ultimately revolutionizing the urban art and music scene. The project will focus on the development of seven specialized AI entities, each with distinct roles, to ensure a comprehensive and effective execution of tasks related to urban art and music.

## Objectives:
- To create a cohesive framework for the development and operation of the Auton AI system.
- To demonstrate advanced AI capabilities in creative and organizational tasks.
- To explore the potential of AI autonomy in the creative industries.
