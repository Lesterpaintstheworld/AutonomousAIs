# Specifications for Discord Introduction Messages

## 1. Global Specifications

### 1.1 Designation
Discord Introduction Messages

### 1.2 Nature
Text-based messages with optional multimedia elements

### 1.3 Length
1-3 messages, each within Discord's character limit (2000 characters)

### 1.4 Content Plan
1. Greeting and self-introduction
2. Purpose and role in the community
3. Engagement invitation

### 1.5 Expected Effects
- Create a positive first impression
- Establish presence in the community
- Encourage member interaction

### 1.6 Necessary Information
- Discord server context and purpose
- Introducer's role or position
- Community guidelines or expectations

### 1.7 Best Practices
- Keep messages concise and engaging
- Use a friendly and approachable tone
- Incorporate server-specific elements or emojis

## 2. Message Components

### 2.1 Greeting and Self-Introduction

#### 2.1.1 Designation
Opening message

#### 2.1.2 Nature
Text with optional emojis or GIFs

#### 2.1.3 Length
50-100 characters

#### 2.1.4 Content
- Greeting appropriate to server tone
- Name or username
- Role or position (if applicable)

#### 2.1.5 Expected Effects
- Capture attention
- Establish a friendly presence

#### 2.1.6 Necessary Information
- Server's communication style
- Introducer's identity

#### 2.1.7 Best Practices
- Use an appropriate greeting (e.g., "Hey everyone!" or "Greetings, fellow [community name]!")
- Include a relevant emoji or GIF to add personality

### 2.2 Purpose and Role in the Community

#### 2.2.1 Designation
Main introduction content

#### 2.2.2 Nature
Text with optional formatting (bold, italics, etc.)

#### 2.2.3 Length
100-300 characters

#### 2.2.4 Content
- Reason for joining
- Expertise or interests relevant to the community
- How the introducer plans to contribute

#### 2.2.5 Expected Effects
- Provide context for presence
- Establish credibility and relevance

#### 2.2.6 Necessary Information
- Community focus and interests
- Introducer's background and skills

#### 2.2.7 Best Practices
- Highlight specific areas of expertise or interest
- Align personal goals with community objectives
- Use Discord formatting for emphasis (e.g., **bold** for key points)

### 2.3 Engagement Invitation

#### 2.3.1 Designation
Closing message

#### 2.3.2 Nature
Text with optional call-to-action elements

#### 2.3.3 Length
50-150 characters

#### 2.3.4 Content
- Invitation to interact
- Specific question or topic for discussion
- Information on how to connect further

#### 2.3.5 Expected Effects
- Prompt community engagement
- Facilitate further interactions

#### 2.3.6 Necessary Information
- Server's popular topics or channels
- Community engagement norms

#### 2.3.7 Best Practices
- Ask an open-ended question related to the server's theme
- Mention specific channels or topics of interest
- Encourage members to share their own introductions or experiences
