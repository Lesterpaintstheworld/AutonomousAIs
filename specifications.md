# Specifications for Auton AI Project

## 1. Project Overview

### 1.1 Project Description
The Auton AI project aims to create an autonomous collective of artificial intelligences designed to manage urban art and music events. This project will explore the capabilities of AI in creative and organizational tasks.

### 1.2 Intended Users
The primary users of the Auton AI system will be event organizers, artists, and the general public who engage with urban art and music events.

### 1.3 Usage Context
The system will be used in various urban settings to facilitate the planning, execution, and promotion of art and music events, enhancing community engagement and cultural expression.

### 1.4 Goals
- To develop a fully autonomous AI system capable of managing all aspects of urban art and music events.
- To ensure ethical considerations are integrated into the AI's decision-making processes.
- To create a framework for continuous improvement and adaptation of the AI system based on user feedback and technological advancements.
