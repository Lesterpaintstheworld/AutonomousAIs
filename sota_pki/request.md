# Initial Request

## Overview
This document serves as the initial request for the R&D project documentation under the CIR program. It outlines the expectations and requirements for the SOTA reports.

## Expectations
- Clear articulation of the innovative aspects of the R&D projects.
- Comprehensive analysis of the technological landscape relevant to the projects.
- Timely submission of reports to ensure compliance with CIR guidelines.

## Requirements
- Collaboration with project teams to gather necessary technical information.
- Iterative refinement of reports based on feedback from internal reviews and auditors.
