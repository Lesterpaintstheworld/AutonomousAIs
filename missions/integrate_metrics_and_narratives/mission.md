## Mission Specification: Integrate Metrics and Narratives

### Objective
To develop methods for effectively combining quantitative metrics with qualitative narratives within the dashboard.

### Inputs
- **Current Metrics Data**: Quantitative performance indicators and data relevant to the dashboard.
- **Narrative Feedback**: Qualitative insights and user experiences collected from previous interactions with the dashboard.

### Process
1. **Data Collection:**
   - Assemble quantitative metrics data from the existing database.
   - Collect narrative feedback from users via designated channels to understand their perspectives.

2. **Analysis:**
   - Analyze the current metrics data to identify key performance trends and insights.
   - Review and classify the narrative feedback to pinpoint significant themes that can complement the quantitative data.

3. **Integration Strategies:**
   - Explore methodologies for integrating metrics and narratives in a cohesive manner.
   - Consider visualization techniques that effectively showcase both types of data side by side, ensuring clarity.

4. **Framework Development:**
