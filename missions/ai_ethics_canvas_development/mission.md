Mission
============
````
This sub-mission aims to collaboratively develop the 'AI Ethics Canvas,' integrating various ethical frameworks and emotional inputs to visualize ethical dilemmas in AI development. Inputs will include contributions from team members regarding ethical principles, emotional triggers, and potential consequences of ethical choices. Outputs will be a dynamic visual representation of the ethical framework, including elements like the 'Public Perception Pulse' and sliders for 'Innovation vs. Regulation.'
````
