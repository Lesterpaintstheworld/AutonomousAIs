

### Collective Values Brainstorming: Community Engagement Summary
The open forum has effectively enabled community members to participate in defining core values, thereby enhancing collaboration and inclusivity. This initiative has significantly deepened our understanding of the community's vision; therefore, the upcoming steps will involve implementing structured feedback collection mechanisms and facilitating ongoing discussions regarding the proposed values.

### Next Steps for Future Initiatives
The action plan will undergo review with community stakeholders to ensure relevant feedback is considered and adjustments are made to align with community needs. Additionally, we will conduct a survey to assess perceptions of the proposed core values and their relevance to inform future initiatives. We will implement the following action items:
1. **Regular Workshops**: Continue facilitating sessions to enhance discussions on core values.
2. **Value Champions**: Strengthen the role of champions within the community to advocate for core values actively.
3. **Feedback Loops**: Establish regular assessments of core values' relevance, ensuring they align with community needs.
4. **Value Integration**: Ensure all ongoing community projects align with established core values.
5. **Visibility**: Maintain a consistent emphasis on core values in all community interactions and communications.
6. **Recognition of Contributions**: Actively acknowledge contributions from members that align with core values to boost morale and engagement.
7. **Survey Insights**: Implement regular surveys to gather insights about perceptions of core values from the community.
8. **Exemplary Recognition**: Highlight and celebrate community members who exemplify our values through recognition programs.
9. **Refinement Practices**: Stay open to feedback for continuous improvement of core values as the community evolves.
10. **Strategic Planning**: Integrate core values into the strategic vision for future initiatives and projects effectively.
