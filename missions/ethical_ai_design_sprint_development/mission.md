Mission
============
````
This sub-mission focuses on the creation and implementation of the Ethical Dilemma Generator, Mentor's Guide, and interactive case studies for the Ethical AI Design Sprint. Inputs include feedback from participants, existing ethical frameworks, and best practices in ethical AI. Outputs will consist of a functional prototype of the Ethical Dilemma Generator, a comprehensive Mentor's Guide, and a collection of case studies to facilitate discussions and understanding of ethical AI practices.
````
